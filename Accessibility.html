Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Owedify is committed to making owedify.com and our digital kits usable by as many people as possible, including people who use screen readers, keyboard navigation, screen magnification, or other assistive technology.

Our goal: we aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. Accessibility is ongoing work, and we keep improving as we learn.

What we do to support access

  • Text and background colors are chosen for readable contrast.
  • The site and kits can be used with a keyboard, and visible focus outlines show where you are.
  • Buttons, links, and form fields have clear labels for screen readers.
  • Pop-ups can be closed with the Escape key and return focus to the page.
  • We respect your "reduce motion" setting and limit animation.
  • Text can be resized in your browser without breaking the layout.

Known limitations

Some documents and older content may not yet fully meet our target. If you run into something that is hard to use, please tell us, and we will help you get what you need and fix the issue.

Using the kits with assistive technology

Each kit runs entirely in your own browser, with no account and no upload. If a step is difficult to complete with your assistive technology, email us and we will walk you through it or send the same information in another format.

Tell us about a problem

If you have trouble accessing any part of owedify.com or a kit, email help@owedify.com. Please include the page or kit, what you were trying to do, and the assistive technology or browser you use. We will respond within a reasonable time and do our best to provide the information or function you need.

Contact

Questions about accessibility? Email help@owedify.com.
