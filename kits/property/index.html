Every county is different.

The deadline is on your notice.

Property tax appeals are local, so the deadline, the form, and the board are different in every county. Many give you a short window from the date your assessment notice was mailed. The kit helps you find your county's exact rule then helps you add the deadline to your calendar.

The savings most people miss

Exemptions stack on top of an appeal.

An appeal lowers your assessed value. Exemptions lower the taxable part separately, so you can claim both. Sometimes a single missed exemption saves more than a won appeal.

Homestead

For the home you live in as your primary residence. The most common one, and often missed.

Senior or disability

Based on age, income, or a qualifying disability. Many counties also offer a value freeze.

Disabled veteran

A service-connected disability can reduce or even eliminate the bill in many places.

Local programs

Freezes, abatements, and circuit breakers that vary by county. The kit helps you ask for every one.

Not sure which you qualify for? The kit drafts a single letter that asks your assessor about every program your property may be eligible for.
